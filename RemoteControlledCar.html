A Project I Made For Students!

Video's coming soon! Participants will learn about the physical properties of radio waves and their uses for telecommunications. They will investigate ways to block radio signals by making a Faraday cage. This knowledge will serve as a building block to discuss the concept of spectrum sovereignty - the need for Indigenous communities to have ownership of the radio spectrum that travel through their land. These issues can affect all types of life throughout the environment. Environmental Scientists have seen bird migration and animal migration change because of cell towers and possibly other things that release spectrum waves. Using a micro-controller connected to 2 motors and wheels, block the signal to and from the reciever.
