M.A.R.C.O

Micro.Adjustable.Robotic.Controller.Offshoot. I designed this little guy using just duct tape, popsicle sticks and zip ties. I used 5 servo motors as a 5 point-axis arm. 5 potieniameters for each movement in a 90* span. This is a good way of showing how resourceful I can be when challenged and a great example of my understanding of electronics, physics and programming. The body is in development still and will be uploaded soon.
